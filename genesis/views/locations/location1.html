Grain Belt Beer Sign Location 1

Location Description

Built in 1941 for $5,000, the Grain Belt Beer bottle cap sign is one of the largest freestanding neon signs in the region.

The “M” inside a circle above “Grain Belt Beer” is the logo for the original brewing company, Minneapolis Brewing.

Location Details

  • The sign is approximately 50 feet wide and 40 feet tall.
  • The sign’s bottle cap design with a red diamond background dates back to 1938
  • Mentioned in The Hold Steady song "Party Pit"
  • Acquired by August Schell Brewing Company on January 31, 2016
