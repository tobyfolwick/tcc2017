U of M Shoe Tree Location 8

Location Description

It's a rite of passage at the University of Minnesota. There are many claimed reasons for why people started doing this (see below) but mostly it's just a tradition at the U of M.

Location Details

  • You throw your shoes in the tree when you get your first F.
  • You throw your shoes in the tree when you lose your virginity.
  • You throw your shoes in the tree when you graduate, to symbolize all the time spent walking around campus.
  • OR You just throw your shoes in the tree
